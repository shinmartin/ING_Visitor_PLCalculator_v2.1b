Life is full of surprises. An ING Personal Loan isn't.

Life is full of surprises, and knowing what's around the corner is important when it comes to your money. A personal loan from ING has a low personalised fixed interest rate, no ongoing fees and no early repayment fees just what you'd expect from Australia's most recommended bank.

Features

Why apply for an ING Personal Loan?

  • Low fixed interest rate

    We've made our lowest ever ING Personal Loan rate available to all applicants in March. Just 6.99% p.a. fixed (7.13% p.a. comparison rate).

  • Pay no ongoing fees

    Keep things simple and money in your pocket with no monthly or annual fees. Establishment fee applies if you don't have an Orange Everyday account.

  • Pay it off faster, no problem

    Flexibility to make extra repayments whenever you like. Want to pay off early? No early repayment fees if you pay the loan ahead of time.

  • Combine and save

    We'll waive the $100 establishment fee on your loan if you have an active Orange Everyday account at the time of your loan application. Don't have an Orange Everyday? You can open one with your ING Personal Loan and save the fee.

  • Same day funds

    If you are an existing ING customer, the money can be received into your Orange Everyday on the same day as you accept your loan offer.

  • Loans from $5,000 to $60,000

    Loan terms of 2-7 years with fortnightly or monthly repayments. Loan terms of 6 or 7 years are only available for loan amounts exceeding $30,000.

How to get started

Ready for ING Personal Loan?

If you want to borrow between $5,000 and $60,000, all you have to do is:

1. Apply

It only takes about 20 minutes to complete our online application form.

2. Accept the contract

While we work through your application, we'll keep you in the loop via email and SMS. If approved, you then review and accept your contract online.

3. Get paid, same day

Receive the money on the same day you accept your loan offer if you're an existing customer, and elect for the money to be transferred to your Orange Everyday account.

Things to know before you apply:
To be eligible to apply for an ING Personal Loan, you must:

Be aged 18+ and have a valid proof of ID – driver’s licence, passport or Medicare

Earn $36,000 or more a year (before tax) with PAYG payslips as your primary source of income

Be an Australian citizen, New Zealand citizen, or a permanent resident of Australia

Maintain an Australian residential address

Have a good credit rating and meet our credit lending guidelines

ING Personal Loans are currently not available to Self-employed and Contractor applicants.

You should have the following supporting documents handy so we can assess your application faster. Examples of supporting documents can include:

PAYG payslips

Proof of super income

Bank statements

Tax returns
